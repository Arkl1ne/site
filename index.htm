Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I track the status of my application?

The status of the application can be tracked in the Personal Account in the "application" section (the login and password from which will be sent to your mail, during registration, or when you first create an application). We will also notify you of the status of your application by email.

Is registration required to make an exchange?

In the BombCriptos service, automatic registration occurs when the first application is created. Upon completion of the exchange, you will be sent an email to log in to your personal account.

Why do you need confirmations in the bitcoin system?

When receiving bitcoin coins, the recipient cannot immediately use them. Once a transfer is made, it is sent to the Bitcoin network for execution and must be included in a block to become confirmed. The process of including a transfer in the found block is called transaction confirmation. Inclusion in 1 block = 1 confirmation, when there are 2 or more such confirmations, the transfer is considered confirmed, and BombCriptos operators, in turn, process the request. This method was introduced to protect against reuse of the same bitcoins.

How long does the exchange take?

The exchange rate is from 1 to 30 minutes, the countdown starts from the moment the payment is received on the application.

Minimum exchange amount

Is it possible to cancel an application?.

If the application has been processed, then the funds are non-refundable. If the application was paid, but was not executed, you need to write to the operator. You will be able to return the funds minus the transfer fee.

How can I track the status of my application?

The status of the application can be found in the Personal account of the service in the item "My applications". In addition, information on the status of the application will automatically be sent to the e-mail address that you indicated at the time of making the application for the exchange.
